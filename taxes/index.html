Taxes

Taxes pay for important things in Massachusetts that will make your trip like no other.

  1. Civic Education Trust Fund: We pay $1,500,000 each year to promote civics education in schools. This means that people you meet in Massachusetts have a much higher chance of knowing about important civics topics.
  2. Massachusetts IT Center: We pay $500,000 a year to operate the Massachusetts information technology center in the city of Chelsea. This keeps IT services for the state running so that you have a great experience here.
  3. School Lunch Program: Every year we pay $5,314,176 to fund school lunch programs. This helps pay for free or reduced cost lunches for schoolchildren.
  4. State Lottery Commision: We spend $86,495,868 a year to operate the State Lottery Commission and our arts library. You can try to win big with our amazingly funded lottery.
